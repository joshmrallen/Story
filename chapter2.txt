'Twas the night before memorial day, and all residents of the town were safely tucked away in their hovels, quarantined from the pandemic ravaging the world ...

The collaborators on the git repository for safely sleeping, snug in their sheets, peacefully dreaming of the culmination of all of their hard work.

The machinations of the alien plot would usurp their code, changing it into a virus that would take over the internet in all but 3 hours, standard earth time measurement....
